Types of loans

Car loan

A car loan is very much essential today for commuting conveniently. It is easy to apply for a car loan online. The interest rates will vary from one bank to another and hence it is important to compare the interest rates before application.

Personal loan

Education loans, wedding loans, medical loans, loans for home renovation, travel loans, shopping loans, etc. are all personal loans. To get such a loan, the applicant has to submit only a few documents like proof of assets, proof of income to prove that he/she has enough assets to repay the loan. Personal loans can be very helpful for replaying small loan amounts.

Home loan

Home loans prove extremely beneficial to finance the purchase of home properties. The interest rate starts from approximately 6.40% p.a. for tenures up to 30 years depending on the credit score of the applicant, monthly income, amount of loan, and other factors.

Two-wheeler loan

A two wheeler loan can help people buy their dream two-wheeler vehicle. The ratio of debt to income, credit score, loan tenure, etc. are some factors that will play a crucial role in determi9ning the loan amount.

Business loan

A business loan can be borrowed for meeting both planned and unplanned business expenses. This unsecured financing is available to partnership firms, private limited companies, self-employed non-professionals, and self-employed professionals.

Credit cards

Credit cards prove handy for people planning to make high-ticket purchases. Having a credit card can make shopping an extremely rewarding experience. Justdial is the right place to find a credit card that perfectly suits different needs and helps users to make the most of their purchases.

Steps in getting loans online

While the steps will vary from one online lender to another, almost all of them follow a standard procedure. Here are the various steps involved in getting a loan online:

People willing to take a loan have to visit the lender's website

They have to choose a type of loan they want to apply for

Click on the apply option

The bank will check the eligibility of the applicant and request the applicant to submit the required KYC documents and income documents

The bank will disburse the loan amount to the bank account of the applicant if they find the applicant to be eligible
